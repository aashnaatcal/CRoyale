Hey there! Want to know how to play my game, Clash Royale?

Some things to know:

- Any players on your side are wearing blue. Any players on the opposite side are wearing red.

- Your goal is to take out the three red towers on the opposite side of the screen. But be careful! It's going to take a lot of energy and manpower to take them down.

- You can drag and drop fighters onto the screen. Each fighter requires a certain amount of energy.

- The amount of energy you currently have can be seen at the very bottom. It's the purple bar. If you don't have enough energy, you can't use fighters and they'll bounce right back when you try to place them onto the screen.

How to win: 

- If you take out all three of the red towers, you'll win the game!

The different fighters: 

- Fighter 1: This is the weakest fighter. It costs two energy bars, and can only survive 10 hits.

- Fighter 2: This is the second strongest fighter. It costs 4 energy bars and can survive 30 hits. 

- Fighter 3: This is the strongest fighter. It costs 6 energy bars and can survive 90 hits.
